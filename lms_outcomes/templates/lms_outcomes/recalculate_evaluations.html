Recalculate Outcome Evaluations

Recalculation Overview

This process will recalculate all outcome evaluations for students based on their current rubric evaluations and the configured calculation methods for each outcome.

What happens during recalculation?

  • All existing outcome evaluations will be updated with new scores
  • Calculations use the current rubric-outcome mappings and weights
  • Each outcome's configured calculation method will be applied
  • Students without any relevant rubric evaluations will not have evaluations created
  • Proficiency levels will be automatically determined based on the calculated scores

Important Considerations

  • This process may take several minutes for large numbers of students and outcomes
  • Existing evaluations will be overwritten with newly calculated values
  • Only outcomes with active rubric-outcome mappings will be calculated
  • You can only recalculate evaluations within your access permissions

Performance-Based Methods
